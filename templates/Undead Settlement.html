Undead Settlement

Undead Settlement is a Location in Dark Souls 3. After receiving the Small Lothric Banner from Emma, the player approaches a cliff and is grabbed and transported by flying gargoyles to the base of the High Wall of Lothric. The decaying ruins are crowded with hordes of undead, but there's also treasure to be found...
