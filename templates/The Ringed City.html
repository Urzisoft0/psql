The Ringed City

The Ringed City is a Location in Dark Souls 3. It is the name of the main location of the second DLC, The Ringed City. A traditional city for the Pygmies, called the Ringed City. Half covered in foliage, the city looks desolated with ancient ruins. A portion of the bridge is curved, creating a wall on the inner side. Countless tombstones line the top of the wall made of rock.
