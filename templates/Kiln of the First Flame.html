Kiln of the First Flame

Kiln of the First Flame is a location in Dark Souls 3. In order to reach this area, the player must first kill all Lords of Cinder and speak to the Fire Keeper in Firelink Shrine. Interacting with the Firelink bonfire will then warp the player to the first bonfire.
